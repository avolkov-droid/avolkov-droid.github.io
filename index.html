About TAPG

TAPG is a leading agro-industrial olive oil producer based in Tunisia, operating two fully automated production lines with a total annual capacity of 7,000 tons of premium extra virgin olive oil. We combine tradition with cutting-edge technology to deliver high-quality products to global markets.

Our Facility

  • Two 1,000 L/h fully automated olive oil lines
  • Integrated bottling, labeling, and packaging system
  • 2,000-ton stainless steel storage with nitrogen sealing
  • In-house quality control lab (acidity, peroxide, UV tests)
  • Industry 4.0 monitoring and temperature control

Product Lines

We offer premium Tunisian olive oil in various formats:

  • Bulk Export: Drums & IBC tanks
  • Private Label: 1L & 5L PET bottles
  • Premium Line: 250ml & 500ml glass bottles
